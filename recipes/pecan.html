Pecan Tomato Pasta

This delicious vegan pasta dish with pecans is unique and sure to please. It makes for a quick, tasty, and healthy meal.

ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Pecans
  • Shallot
  • Garlic
  • Kale
  • Fresh basil and parsley
  • Oregano and red pepper flakes

How to Make Vegetarian Pecan Tomato Pasta:

  1. Boil and drain pasta according to the package instructions. Cook to al dente.
  2. Add pecans to a food processor and pulse to break apart into small pieces.
  3. Add sun dried tomato oil, chopped heirloom tomatoes, chopped sun dried tomatoes, minced shallot, minced garlic, oregano and red pepper flakes to a deep frying pan. Sauté for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the kale and pecans. Cook until wilted for 5 minutes.
  5. Add noodles, ½ the basil and parsley. Stir in the salt and pepper and cook for 5 minutes to heat through.
  6. Garnish with remaining basil and parsley and serve immediately.
