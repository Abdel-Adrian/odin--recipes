Why do I love this baked ziti recipe? This is one of my favorite Italian recipes because it is simple, makes a large serving, and is perfect for sharing with family and friends.

ingredients:

  • 2 pounds ground beef round (at least 85% lean)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 48 ounces spaghetti sauce
  • 16 ounces mini ziti noodles
  • 12 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese shredded

  1. Brown the ground beef and onion together. Drain off the fat.
  2. Add the spaghetti sauce and stir to mix.
  3. Cook mini ziti noodles according to package directions.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  5. Spray a 9x13 pan with cooking spray.
  6. Spread half the noodles in the bottom of the dish.
  7. Layer the provolone cheese, sour cream and then half the sauce on top.
  8. Add the rest of the ziti.
  9. Sprinkle 6 ounces of shredded mozzarella over top the ziti.
  10. Top with the remaining sauce, parmesan cheese and the remaining mozzarella.
  11. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
  12. If you'd like the top to get a touch of brown, broil the casserole for 2-3 minutes on high. Watch carefully!
